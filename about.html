About

Nourish your body
awaken your spirit

Hi, I’m Valeria Dekusar — a certified yoga teacher with over 10 years of dedicated practice.

My journey began as a personal exploration of well-being and has grown into a lifelong passion for guiding others.

In my classes, you’ll find a balance of movement, breath, and mindfulness — designed for all levels and all bodies. My goal is simple: to help you feel more connected, grounded, and alive.

I believe in a balanced practice that nurtures both the body and the mind. That’s why I combine two powerful methods: Yoga and Power Plate training, each bringing its own unique benefits.”
